Integrate Odoo with Office 365 Connector

Office 365 Odoo Connector provides the opportunity for Office 365 users to sync their contacts, e-mails, calendars and tasks between Odoo and Office 365. This connector helps Odoo users to have seamless integration between the two platforms.

Odoo to Office 365 Import Operations

  • Import/Update Contacts from Office 365 to Odoo.
  • Import/Update Calendar's event from office 365 to Odoo.
  • Import Emails from Office 365 (Inbox & Send Items) to Odoo.
  • Import/Update Tasks from Office 365 to Odoo.
  • All these things can also be done by auto scheduler.

Office 365 to Odoo Export Operations

  • Export/Update Contacts from Odoo to Office 365.
  • Export/Update Calendar's events from Odoo to Office 365.
  • Export/Update Tasks from Odoo to Office 365.
  • All these things can also be done by auto scheduler.

Multi User Synchronization

  • All these import/export operations mentioned about can be performed in a multi user environment. Where multiple Office 365 users of an organization will be configured in Odoo and every individual can sync their data between Odoo and Office 365 by logging in Odoo from their Odoo account.

Office 365 Configuration Menu

Microsoft Azure Authentication

  • Create a Microsoft Azure account on the Azure portal.
  • Create a new resource ‘App registrations’.

Microsoft Azure App Registration

  • Click on 'New registration'

Register App

  • Add your app name and choose the account type.
  • Click ‘Register’.

Azure App

  • After registering your app you can see your ‘Application (client) ID’

Redirect URL

  • Click on 'Add Redirect URL'.

Redirect URL

  • Click on 'Add a platform'
  • Click ‘Register’.

Redirect URL

  • Select 'Web' as your redirect URL platform.
  • Click ‘Register’.

Redirect URL

  • Under the heading of 'Redirect URL' add your Odoo URL.
  • Click ‘Register’.

Client Secret

  • Go to 'Certificates & Secrets'
  • Click on ‘New client secret’.

Client Secret

  • Add description and expiry of your secret key.

Client Secret

  • The key under 'Value' is your client secret key.

Office 365 Settings

  • After completing the process above go to Office 365 setting page.
  • Copy 'Application ID' and 'Secret ID' ftom Azure portsl and enter here.
  • Click on save.

Office 365 Login

  • You will see login Office 365 button over there.
  • Click Login Button, it will open Office 365 login page
  • After successfully login in Office 365 account you will be able to get access of your Office 365 Account.

Office 365 Login

  • Enter your Office 365 email id.

Office 365 Login

  • Enter your Office 365 password.

Office 365 Login

  • Check 'Consent on behalf of your organisation'
  • Click on accept to accept permissions.

Office 365 Login

  • After successfully login in Office 365 account you will see this message.
  • The page will be redirected back to Odoo.

Office 365 Import Operation

  • After Successful login of Office 365 account, here you can see the buttons to perform operation by selecting these buttons you will able to import and export data between Office 365 and Odoo.
  • In import contacts you can import based on contacts category.
  • If category isn't selected contacts of all categories will be imported.
  • In import calendars you can import based on calendar type and category.
  • If category isn't selected calendar of all categories will be imported.
  • You can activate auto scheduler from here. If you want to perform manual sync you have the option to import data based on date range specified here.

Office 365 Export Operation

  • Similarly export operations can be performed.
  • You can activate auto scheduler from here. If you want to perform manual sync you have the option to export data based on date range specified here.

Office 365 Emails

  • Office 365 emails are shown on the contact page.
  • From here you can send emails to your contacts. The emails you will receive will also be shown here after importing them from the Office 365 configuration menu.

Office 365 To Do Section

  • In order to view 'To Do' task you can click on 'To Do' on the navigation menu.
  • Here you can see your 'To Do' tasks in both kanban and list view.

Office 365 Import/Export Logs

  • In order to view logs you can click on 'Logs' on the navigation menu.
  • Here you can see your logs of import/export operations.
