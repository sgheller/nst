Sale Delivery Date

Allows you to generate the delivery orders based on delivery date

Features
  • It can group the deliveries of same same date in a delivery order.
  • It will generate different delivery for products that have different delivery dates.

Sample demonstration how the Sale Delivery Date works :

1. Setting the Delivery Dates :
  • Here you can set the delivery dates of orders in the order line.
2. Confirm the Sale Order :
  • After entering the details for the sale order, confirm it.
3. After Confirmation :
  • After confirmation, the delivery orders are created based on the product's delivery date.
4. Delivery Order List(Transfers) :
  • Here all the generated deliveries or transfers are listed.
5. Sample Delivery Order One :
  • Here the products have same delivery date.
5. Sample Delivery Order Two :
  • Here the products have same delivery date.
